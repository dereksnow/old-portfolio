About Me

Hi! I'm a Front-End developer experienced in creating responsive web apps. I got my start in the software industy as an embedded systems engineer writing software in C and various assembly languages, but over the past few years I've been doing most of my coding in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I also taught computer science as a university instructor for eight years and I still enjoy sharing what I've learned through video and written tutorials. Check out some of my projects below.
